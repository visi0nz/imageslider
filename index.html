Lofoten Islands

This image shows the Fredvang Bridges on the Lofoten Islands in Norway. The bridges connect the islands of Flakstadøya and Moskenesøya, offering a spectacular view of the surrounding mountains and sea. The Lofoten Islands are known for their dramatic landscape and small fishing villages.

ICELAND

Hvítserkur

The image shows Hvítserkur, a famous basalt rock formation on the north coast of Iceland. It is located near the Vatnsnes Peninsula in the Húnaflói Bay area. The shape of the rock, which resembles an animal or a mythological figure, and the rough waves of the Atlantic are characteristic of this site.

TÜRKIYE

Kapadokya

This image depicts Kapadokya, a region in central Türkiye. The landscape is famous for its unique rock formations, fairy chimneys, and extensive hot air balloon rides, which offer stunning views of the valleys and ancient cave dwellings. Kapadokya, particularly the town of Göreme, is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a popular tourist destination.

GERMANY

Neuschwanstein Castle

The image shows Neuschwanstein Castle in Bavaria, Germany. It is located near the town of Füssen in the Allgäu region, surrounded by the Alps and forests. In the background, you can see the Alpsee lake and Hohenschwangau Castle, which are typical of this area. Neuschwanstein is one of the most famous castles in the world and served as the inspiration for the Sleeping Beauty Castle in Disneyland.

SWITZERLAND

Oeschinensee

This image depicts the Oeschinensee, a stunning lake located in the Bernese Oberland region of Switzerland. The lake is surrounded by the majestic peaks of the Swiss Alps, including the Blüemlisalp and Doldenhorn mountains. The traditional wooden chalet and the turquoise water are characteristic features of this area, which is a popular destination for hikers and nature lovers.

PERU

Machu Picchu

This image shows Machu Picchu, an ancient Incan city located in Peru. Situated in the Andes Mountains at an elevation of about 2,430 meters above sea level. Machu Picchu, built in the 15th century, is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most iconic archaeological sites in the world, known for its stone architecture and dramatic mountainous setting.
